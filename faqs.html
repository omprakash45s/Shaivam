General questions

Admissions at Shaivam Early Years Preschool in Delhi begin from 15 months of age. We offer three programs designed for early childhood development: Little Steps Together (15–24 months), Little Explorers (2–3 years), and Pathfinders (3–4 years).

Shaivam Early Years follows a child-centered, experiential learning approach inspired by Montessori and play-based preschool education. As a modern early learning center in Delhi, we focus on nurturing curiosity, confidence, and independence through hands-on exploration.

At Shaivam Early Years, we maintain a low teacher-to-child ratio, which allows us to provide personalized attention to every child. This approach is essential for quality early childhood education and preschool learning in Delhi.

Yes. Our preschool program follows a structured yet flexible daily routine that includes circle time, sensory activities, creative play, storytelling, and outdoor exploration — creating a balanced early learning experience for preschoolers.

As an early years learning center in Delhi, we focus strongly on emotional development. Through collaborative play, guided interactions, and a nurturing environment, children learn empathy, communication, and self-confidence.

Admissions and enrollment

The admission process involves a visit to the school, a brief interaction with the child and parents, and submission of the required documents. Please contact our admission office for detailed step-by-step guidance.

Typically, the following documents are required:

  • Child’s birth certificate
  • Passport-size photographs of the child and parents
  • Parent identification details
Additional documents may be requested if required.

Yes. Parents looking for a preschool in Delhi are encouraged to visit Shaivam Early Years to explore our learning spaces, understand our philosophy, and meet our educators.

Programs and curriculum

Our curriculum combines play-based learning, Montessori-inspired methods, and experiential education, making Shaivam Early Years a thoughtfully designed preschool program in Delhi focused on holistic development.

Children at Shaivam Early Years develop important school readiness skills, including language development, early numeracy, creativity, social skills, independence, and problem-solving through fun play based learning.

Yes. Our preschool program includes sensory play, art, music, storytelling, messy play, and hands-on exploration, which are essential for brain development in early childhood.

Learning at Shaivam Early Years happens through play, exploration, stories, music, experiments, and interactive activities, making it one of the most engaging experiential preschools in Delhi.

Yes. We regularly observe and document each child’s development and share updates with parents through meetings, feedback sessions, and progress reports.

Communication guidelines

Policies and procedures

Children’s safety is our highest priority. The school maintains secure entry systems, trained staff supervision, child-safe infrastructure, and emergency protocols.

School timings vary slightly depending on the program. Detailed timing information is shared with parents during the admission process.

Children must be dropped off and picked up by authorized adults only. Parents are requested to follow the school’s safety guidelines during arrival and departure.

We follow strict hygiene practices, including regular sanitization of classrooms, toys, and learning materials, along with maintaining clean and safe spaces for children.

At Shaivam Early Years, we follow a positive and respectful guidance approach. Instead of punishment, we focus on helping children understand emotions, make better choices, and develop self-regulation skills.
