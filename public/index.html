Our Academic Programs

Specialized Early Learning Programs Designed for Growth

Little Steps Together
(Parent-Child Program)

Age Group: 15 months to 24 months

This program serves as a gentle bridge between home and school. Designed for toddlers and their caregivers, it focuses on the sensory exploration and social bonding.

Little Explorers (PRE-nursery)

Age Group: 2 to 3 years

This program focus shifts toward independence and discovery. Through a play-based curriculum, children begin to develop fundamental language and motor skills.

PATHFINDERS (nursery)

Age Group: 3 years onwards

Building a strong academic and social foundation. As children grow more vocal, we introduce interactive literacy and numeracy to prepare them for future success.

Our Mission

To nurture the child — physically, cognitively, socially, and emotionally — by blending Montessori inspired teaching to align with NEP 2020. We foster curiosity, confidence, and emotional awareness to build a strong foundation for lifelong learning into primary education.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing early learning ecosystem where every child feels valued, respected, and inspired to explore their individuality. We aim to shape confident, curious, and compassionate learners prepared for a global future while staying rooted in strong values.

Parent-Child Program admissions typically begin from 15 months, PRE-nursery from 2 years, and Nursery from 3 years onwards, as per curriculum guidelines.

We maintain strict safety protocols, CCTV monitoring, child-safe infrastructure, trained staff supervision, and hygiene standards to ensure every child feels secure and cared for.

Teachers share regular updates through parent communication apps, WhatsApp groups, circulars, and scheduled parent-teacher meetings.

Generally required documents include: Birth Certificate, Child Passport Size Photos, Parent ID Proof ,Address Proof, Previous School Records (if applicable)

We follow a gradual settling process, allowing children to adapt at their own pace through engaging activities, emotional support, and regular parent communication.

Our teachers are provided an extensive training by an early childhood expert on a monthly basis and are evaluated by a third party on a quarterly basis to ensure every child receives the highest standard of personalized care
