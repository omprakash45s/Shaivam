Our programs

Pre-Nursery Program

Age: 2 years onwards
Time: 9:30 am – 12:00 pm

A gentle introduction to structured learning through play, music, and storytelling, we focus on building a child’s vocabulary and social confidence through interactive group sessions. Our well-designed curriculum offers a balanced combination of academic and play-based activities, helping children engage and explore their environment.

We nurture natural curiosity by encouraging hands-on discovery and creative expression. By fostering a warm, inclusive atmosphere, we ensure every child feels secure enough to take their first steps toward independence, developing a lifelong love for learning alongside their very first friends.

Nursery Program

Age: 3 years onwards
Time: 9:30 am – 12:00 pm

Our play-based learning approach builds essential language, motor, and social development skills. This stage focuses on more structured activities that effectively prepare children for formal schooling. The objective is to provide motivating and inspiring experiences within smaller class sizes, ensuring 100% individual attention for every child.

Beyond academics, we emphasize emotional resilience and problem-solving. Through collaborative projects and sensory exploration, students learn to navigate new challenges with confidence. We create a supportive launchpad where curiosity meets structure, ensuring your child transitions to their next educational chapter with joy and readiness.

Junior KG Programs

Age: 4 years onwards
Time: 9:30 am – 12:00 pm

We provide a strong foundation in language, numbers, and social skills through interactive learning. By introducing more complex concepts in a way that remains fun and engaging, we bridge the gap between curiosity and academic mastery. Our program gives children the freedom to learn, play, and explore, ensuring every child embarks on a unique educational growth journey tailored to their pace.

To further support this development, we integrate critical thinking and creative arts, encouraging students to ask "why" and "how." Our dedicated educators foster an environment where mistakes are viewed as milestones, building the self-assurance needed for future success.
