Our programs

Little Steps Together (Parent-Child Program)

Age: 15 months to 24 months

Our Parent–Child Program is a gentle first step into the world of early learning. Designed for toddlers aged 15 to 24 months, this program allows children to explore sensory play, music, movement, art, and early social experiences alongside a parent. In a safe and nurturing environment, we focus on strengthening bonding, building language skills, and developing early confidence — making the transition into independent learning smooth and joyful.

Little Explorers (PRE-nursery)

Age: 2 to 3 years

Our Playschool program encourages curiosity, independence, and joyful discovery. Through experiential learning, hands-on activities, storytelling, pretend play, and creative exploration, children begin to develop early literacy, numeracy, communication, and social skills. We create a structured yet flexible environment where children learn to express themselves, build friendships, and grow in confidence every day.

Enroll Now

PATHFINDERS (nursery)

Age: 3 years onwards

The Nursery program lays strong academic and emotional foundations for formal schooling. With a balanced blend of structured learning and play-based exploration, children are introduced to pre-reading skills, early writing readiness, number concepts, problem-solving, and independent thinking. Our focus remains on holistic development — nurturing confident, curious learners who are ready for their next big step.
