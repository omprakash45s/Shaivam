Let's Start Your Child's Bright Learning Journey

Contact Us

Our Playgroup Program is designed for children aged 2 to 3 years, providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where little learners can explore and grow. Through a combination of structured activities and free play, children develop essential early skills such as social interaction, language, motor coordination, and emotional awareness.

Our experienced educators focus on fostering curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child, encouraging them to themselves and discover the world around them. This program emphasizes gentle guidance, hands-on learning, and playful exploration to build a strong foundation for future learning while making every day enjoyable and engaging for young minds.

Why parents love this program

Because it offers a perfect balance of fun, safety, early learning, and caring support—helping little ones feel comfortable and excited about coming to school every day.

Playgroup program highlights

Our Playgroup Program offers a warm, nurturing environment where children aged 2-3 years begin their learning journey. Key highlights include hands-on activities that promote creativity and curiosity, development of basic social and communication skills, and guided play that enhances motor coordination and problem-solving abilities.

  • Safe, friendly, and engaging classroom environment
  • Caring educators who offer individual attention
  • Guide activity to support early motor development
  • Introduction to color shape and simple concepts
